Welcome to KelvSec! This is my place to share cybersecurity and tech projects, jot down things I learn and document the occasional rabbit hole.

I'll be posting project write-ups, useful commands, lessons from things that did not quite go to plan and anything else I think people might enjoy or find helpful.

There will be plenty of practical bits along the way, including readable code examples:

